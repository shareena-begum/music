INDIAN MUSIC BLOG

SITAR

The sitar is a plucked stringed instrument, originating from the Indian subcontinent, used in Hindustani classical music.

The instrument is balanced between the player's left foot and right knee. The hands move freely without having to carry any of the instrument's weight.

TAMBURA

The tanpura is a long-necked plucked string instrument, originating from India, found in various forms in Indian music.

It does not play melody but rather supports and the melody of another instrument or singer by providing a continuous harmonic bourdon or drone.
